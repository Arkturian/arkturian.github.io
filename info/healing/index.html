T-11.8.5 This is not a course in the play of ideas, but in their practical application.

P-in.1 Psychotherapy is the only form of therapy there is. Since only the mind can be sick, only the mind can be healed.

There is a difference between knowing the path and walking the path. --The Matrix movie

So you've got yourself familiar with the course, your doubts on if it's true are fading, and now you are willing to make the next big step -- move from theory to practice. And although this step is natural in any learning, with ACIM it isn't easy because most of the beliefs the book claims we have are deeply unconscious, we are not aware of them. Just take a look at chapter 19 where it talks about The Fear of God. Or maybe even more practical example, the subject on of all sickness to be in the mind. Who on Earth is aware of that? Most people believe they love God. Just as they believe that diseases come from anything but their own mind.

What I found is that all of those unconscious blocks, fears and grievances get (re)injected into our mind at this plane in our early childhood, from 0 to 7. We do that to ourselves from the higher level and then we cut our memory off about it, just like in the Memento movie. So that now when we read the Course's Workbook lesson 31 "I am never the victim of the world I see" we get totally surprised and even protest, because this statement sounds totally ridiculous to us. That's where I found regression sessions to be one of the most helpful tools for experiencing the theoretical foundation presented in ACIM. Here is a video with lots of examples on what results people get when they heal their childhoods' grievances from the unconscious:

From my experience, regression works best when you have a "spring board" -- some painful issue -- relationships, sickness, weight, finances, anxiety etc -- some repeating situation that bothers you but which you cannot solve by other means. Eventually it all boils down to ACIM's genuine forgiveness, which all Course's students have heard about, yet as with any studying to learn it in theory and to realize it in experience is not quite the same thing.

This is your chance to dive deeper into the Course to put it in practice and to join with your brother for the unified purpose of joy, use it ;)
