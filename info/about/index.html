Hello! My name is Igor. I'm studying A Course in Miracles and sometimes I write notes or record youtube videos about it.

I've been using a new tool http://hypothes.is to do that, it allows writing notes on any web page on the Internet. But when the number of notes has increased, I decided to build this website to have access to all of the notes from one place.

Also I like to have a random quote at the end of the study groups sessions, some students call it ACIM Fortune Cookies, but I couldn't find a resource where I could have them online.

As a result of all this, here you can read my notes about the Course and you can have ACIM Fortune Cookies for all of your study group's members, enjoy! :)
