T-1.I "The meaning of miracles"

ACIM is not your yet another esoteric book, a bunch of which you're used to read each month. A lot of things in it remain unknown for you awhile. Don't push it, don't try to squeeze the understanding from it, but allow it to reveal itself for you instead.

The guiding rope for you to navigate this section is knowing: miracles are not some magic tricks. Behind the word "A Miracle", the "Genuine Forgiveness" process rests. And yet this process of forgiveness is not what you may think of it for now.

Forgiveness is the key to happiness. W-121
