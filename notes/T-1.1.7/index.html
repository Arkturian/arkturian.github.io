T-1.1.7 "Miracles are everyone's right, but purification is necessary first."

Fear not the images related to this word your memories have brought. Recall what Author said in introduction: removing blocks to love is all this course's concerned.

Your task is not to seek for love, but merely to seek and find all of the barriers within yourself that you have built against it. T-16.4.6
