T-1.1.16 "Miracles are teaching devices for demonstrating it is as blessed to give as to receive."

Please do not use this statement to justify a charity of any kind. The author speaks about the level of the Mind. When a musician shares his song with others, it hardly means for him it's lost; extending makes it stronger and gives him opportunities he didn't have before. The course takes this idea and brings it to the level where you will be amazed for sure.

A major learning goal this course has set is to reverse your view of giving, so you can receive ... God's gifts will never lessen when they are given away. They but increase thereby. W-105

All that I give is given to myself. W-126
